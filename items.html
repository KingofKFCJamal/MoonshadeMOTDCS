Items

Welcome, and let's get started, players!

Like all Roleplay servers, having items is an essential thing to enjoy and survive in roleplay. There are a various set of items in this server. You can get items from drops, trading, unboxing crates, or buying them. Here a list of items you can get from the server.

  • Gun Shop:

    Sells all available weapons that are included in CS:GO.

  • Locksmith:

    Sells locks, lockbreakers, doorhacks, lockpicks, and cuffsaw.

  • Goodie Shop:

    Sells rare items that are in the server.

  • Furniture Shop:

    Sells furnitures that can be used to decorate your house.

  • Liquor Shop:

    Sells alcohol that you can drink and wipe away your bad memories.

  • Medical Supplies:

    Sells Armour and Medkits to protect yourself and heal your wounded friend.

  • Spy Vendor:

    Sells Spy-related items so you can become the next James Bond.

  • HintText Vendor:

    You can buy these items to change the color of your HintText.

  • Illegal Vendor:

    Sells items so you can make money, but be warned that you get crime from these items.

Drops

In this server, you can get in-game items from drops by playing on the server. To tell if you gotten a drop, look in chat and this should appear...

Crates

There are a series of crates in this server which you can unlock with a key from the Goodies Shop to get rare items.

  • To get a crate, you have to wait for it to drop.
  • Next, you use the key you purchased and use it and select the crate you want to unbox.
  • Wait 5 seconds to reveal... what you got.
  • There are also unusual items which when they are unboxed, it will alerts everyone in the server.
  • To see what you might get in a crate, use the crate and a page will pop up.
