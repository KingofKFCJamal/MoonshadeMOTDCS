Interface and Controls

Interface

  • 1.Mini-Map/Radar

    This helps you see your position on the map, other player's positions, and businesses.

  • 2.Cash

    Shows the current amount of money you are carrying.

  • 3.HUD Menus

    HUD Menus are used to display options for you when interacting with NPCs or your inventory.

  • 4.Hint Text

    These are used for displaying information when in a certain zone or when looking at an entity like a door, a player, a NPC, or when a certain event is triggered.

  • 5.Scoreboard

    This displays certain information that are related to the server. Look at the other parts for a better understanding.

  • 5A.Kills

    Kills on this server are the hours you have accumulated by playing in the server.

  • 5B.Assists

    Assists on this server is your wage, which you get every minute. You can increase this by playing more on the server.

  • 5C.Deaths

    Deaths on this server is the timer that counts down every seconds until your next wage.

  • 5D.Score

    Score on this server is your crime. You get crime by killing players and doing illegal activities in the server. When you hit 800 crime, a cop can cuff you and might jail you. When you hit 2,500 crime, you will have a bounty on your head and a tracer will show up to every players leading to you. Gaining more crime will increase the bounty. If you are killed with a bounty, you will be automatically jailed.

Control

Action Keys

  • E -Uses doors, interacts with NPCs, and other player-related actions.
  • , -Prints your statistics to your chat.
  • R with Knife out -Access your inventory.
