Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome, and let's get started, players!

If you are new to the server or a returning player, here a few answers to questions that are asked alot in the server:

  • Q: How do I access my inventory/items?

    A: Select your knife and press R on your keyboard.

  • Q: How do I interact with a NPC?

    A: Press E on any NPCs to use their services.

  • Q: How do I get a job on the server?

    A: Go to any Duke Nukem NPCs on the map and press E to select through a various of jobs available in this gamemode.

  • Q: How do I make money on the server?

    A: There are multiple ways to make money in the server. You can become a Counterfeiter or a Drug Dealer to make a large sum of money, or rob NPCs by pressing SHIFT twice, or pick up the money dropped on the grounds to make quick cash. There are still more ways to make money than just these.

  • Q: How do I buy a house?

    A: To buy a house, you can type /buydoor or press R with your knife out while looking at the door to buy a house. Be sure that you have enough money in your bank before attempting to buy it. Also after buying the house, you can press SHIFT while looking at the door to lock and unlock the door. To rename your door, type /mydoormess with a message after it.

  • Q: How do I mute annoying players?

    A: Type /sm theirname to self-mute the player in questioning. If you would like to unmute the player, type /su theirname.

  • Q: How do I get cop/admin in the server?

    A: To earn cop or admin in the server, you have to apply on the forums and have a require amount of hours on the server. Cop applicants required to have 30 hours and Admin applicants required to have 100 hours before applying. To check your hours in the server, look at your scoreboard and your Kills will be your hours.
