Recently there has been mass confusion about the following rule:

"Do not use any external program or modification to gain you an advantage in the game"
"Do not exploit glitches found in the server. Report any bugs or bug abuse, failure to do so will result in an immediate multi-day ban. "
"Do not evade bans with different steam accounts or proxies."
"Do not buy player's RP money with Real Life currencies (PayPal, Bitcoin, etc). If you get scammed by this, we will not take action on this."

To be clear, what is not allowed in our community includes but is not limited to:

  • Playing with multiple accounts at the same time as one human to gain an advantage against other players

  • Supporting users who take advantage of the above clarifications

  • Making full use or benefiting from the practice of an external program or modification to your game such as macros, cheats, etc.

If you are taking action in any of the above activities, we request that you terminate them immediately. If for any reason you are confused about the clarifications please do not hesitate to reply to this thread. Our goal is to ensure the fairest economic environment as possible at this point. We will continue to monitor the actions of certain players, and may reevaluate if further actions need to be taken to ensure balance in the gameplay. Thanks
Hello there, players. These are the rules you have to follow.
 
Server Rules

General Discipline Procedure: Warn -> Ban for 10 Minutes -> Ban for 1 Day -> Permenent Ban | Evading punishments like mute, gag, and ban will results in harsher punishments.
Player Rules:
  • Respect all players, no slandering. Calling someone out is not disrespect.
  • Do not promote slow-hacking. This is when you tell someone to do a malicious command or hotkey such as "unbindall", "exit", "demos", etc. Zero tolerance.
  • Do not complain about anything in the server. If you have a problem, add one of the admins and talk to them in private, or visit our forums we are setting up.
  • Do not spam the chat or yell/scream in the microphone. This includes micspam with music.
  • Do not exploit glitches found in the server. Report any bugs or bug abuse, failure to do so will result in an immediate multi-day ban (do not buy only subdoors as this is an exploit).
  • Do not use any external program or modification to gain you an advantage in the game, this is a global rule in every server regardless.
  • Do not evade bans with different steam accounts or proxies.
  • Evading cuffs by disconnecting or switching jobs will results in a 10 minutes VIP.
  • Do not ever kill a new player for no set reason. Doing so will result in a penalty in money. Another exception is players with bounties, or immensely high crime.
  • Mass RDMing is NOT allowed...(i.e. non defensively killing 10 times in 5 minutes)
    If you have a problem with your punishement, bring this up to the council/superiors, do not confront the punisher directly, this may result in more Justified punishments.
